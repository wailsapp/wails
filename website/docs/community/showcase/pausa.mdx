[Pausa](https://github.com/yuseferi/pausa) is a native-feeling macOS break reminder for developers and knowledge workers. It features configurable short and long break schedules, smart busy detection (microphone, media playback, browser video pages), native AppKit overlay panels that render on top of fullscreen Spaces, and wellness guidance with breathing exercises and stretch tips. Pausa lives in your menu bar and automatically pauses the break timer during meetings and calls.
