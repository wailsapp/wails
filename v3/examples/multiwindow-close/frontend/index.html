Repro steps (observe in App Exposé)
  1. Click Open child window.
  2. In the child window, click Close (after Current) or Close (using Current).
  3. Open App Exposé: Control + ↓.
  4. Observe: the child window may still appear as a thumbnail, even though it “closed”.
Note: the Go window list above may show the child as closed/removed.
